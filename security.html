Our Security Services

What We Provide

01

Manned Guarding

Professional on-site security personnel to protect your premises and maintain a secure environment around the clock. Our guards are trained to deter, detect, and respond to security threats with professionalism.

  • 24/7 static guard deployment
  • Professionally trained, uniformed officers
  • Access control & visitor management
  • Incident reporting & documentation
02

Door Supervisors

Trained staff to manage entry points and ensure safety at venues and events. Our door supervisors are experienced in crowd management, conflict resolution, and maintaining safe environments for guests and staff.

  • Door Supervisor trained & certified
  • ID & age verification
  • Conflict de-escalation trained
  • Search & screening procedures
03

Event Security

Reliable crowd management and event protection services for concerts, festivals, corporate events, and private functions. We tailor our security plans to every event's unique requirements.

  • Crowd management specialists
  • Pre-event security planning
  • Perimeter & access control
  • Emergency response coordination
04

CCTV Monitoring

24/7 remote CCTV monitoring to detect, record, and respond to any suspicious activity. Our trained operators monitor multiple sites simultaneously, providing real-time intelligence and immediate escalation.

  • 24/7 remote monitoring
  • Real-time incident escalation
  • Digital footage & reporting
  • Integration with on-site guarding
05

Mobile Patrols

Regular patrol services to secure large or multiple locations cost-effectively. Our mobile patrol officers conduct scheduled and random checks to deter criminal activity and provide rapid response.

  • Scheduled & random patrols
  • Multi-site coverage
  • Keyholding & alarm response
  • GPS tracked & fully logged
